Success Stories.

Visit

Co-winner of Conquest 2017, VISIT is an AI-driven chatbox that analyzes patient symptoms and history, to connect them to right medical professionals. They have received funding by Co-founders of Twitter, Snapdeal and Blue Jeans.

Social Cops

Winner of Eureka! 2013 (Asia’s largest business plan competition), Social cops solves critical problems faced by society using data intelligence. They have been working closely with the government. They have also won YourStory Digital India Award for High Impact Startup in 2017.

Thinkerbell Labs

Winner of Conquest 2016, Thinkerbell lab’s flagship project Annie is the world’s first Self-Learning Braille Device for the visually impaired. They are also the 2nd runner up in Global Social Venture Competition 2018 Out of 550 teams from 60+ countries.

Swagene

Winner of “Masterplan 2015” IIM Ahmedabad B plan competition, ”Empresario 2016” IIT Kharagpur’s Business model competition and Co-winner of “Conquest 2015”, Swagene is changing the face of healthcare by suggesting personalized medication, surgeries, and procedures to regain health based on patient genes. They have also won many other awards like NASSCOM and CII Startup of the Year.

GameZop

Gamezop is a destination for casual games where users can play games without downloading apps and also share the games with friends over WhatsApp or Facebook as playable weblinks. It’s Co-founder Yashah Agarwal represented India in Theil summit in 2015. They have raised 2.4 crore INR in seed funding.
